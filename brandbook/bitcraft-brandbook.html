11 — Guidelines

Usage Rules

✓ Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the smallest bar element.

✓ Minimum Size

Do not use the logo smaller than 24px in height for digital, or 10mm for print applications.

✗ Don't Stretch

Never distort the logo proportions. Always scale uniformly.

✗ Don't Recolor

Use only approved color combinations. Don't apply gradients or effects to the logo.
